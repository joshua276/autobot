How to use autobot

  1. Enter username and password.
  2. After successful login, the user can access his/her dashboard.
  3. View the "rule violation" notice in the messages section of the portal & also view a new message on the registered email id & SMS on the mobile number.
  4. The message will usually be for any updates or also due to violations. In case the user receives a rule violation notice, he/she is expected to pay fine online on the payment portal.
